About Me

I am so excited to be on the path to becoming a full-stack developer. I didn't like coding when I was in college 19 years ago. People told me that coding is boring, headache, and we have to disconnect ourselves from other people! After I have worked in different roles in IT and telecommunication industry for many years, I found that there are so many business problems that are getting resolved with work-around solutions. It would be awesome if I can write codes to develop the solutions to those problems.

I have learned that coding is fun! It helped me connect to other developers in the community to learn from each other. Coding can make my ideas become real. I can help people solve their business problem. And I love helping people. I am not good at talking but I love to be around with people listening to them talking.

I am also excited to be a role model for my kids about learning code. I hope that my kids will love coding, and they can develop some applications for their studies or some games for their entertainment.
